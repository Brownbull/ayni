## 3. Pricing Models That Work in LATAM

### Payment Methods - CRITICAL for Chile

**Chile Payment Preferences:**
- **Credit/Debit cards** (primary method - 44% in similar markets)
- **Digital wallets**: Mercado Pago, PayPal, MACH
- **Multi-currency support**: Display in Chilean Peso (CLP) + USD
- **Local payment gateways**: Partner with EBANX or dLocal

**Key Insight:** 80% of cards used in LATAM are LOCAL cards - you MUST integrate local payment processors.
[Source: Chargeback Gurus, LATAM Payment Methods 2025]

### Pricing Models that Win in LATAM

**1. Freemium Model**
- Conversion rate: **2-5%** (industry standard)
- Most conversions happen in **first 30 days**
- Works well for PLG (product-led growth)
- **Critical:** Free tier must show immediate value
[Source: First Page Sage, SaaS Freemium Conversion Rates 2025]

**2. Tiered Per-Company Pricing** (Most popular for SMBs)
- **Starter**: $20-50/month
- **Professional**: $50-100/month
- **Business**: $100-300/month
- SMBs prefer **simple, transparent pricing** (54% preference)
[Source: Monetizely, SaaS Pricing Benchmarks 2025]

**3. Usage-Based Pricing** (Growing trend)
- 38% of SaaS companies using this in 2025
- Great for analytics (charge per data volume/queries)
- **10-20% faster revenue growth** than fixed pricing
[Source: Monetizely, SaaS Pricing Benchmarks 2025]

### LATAM-Specific Pricing Strategies

✅ **Purchasing Power Parity (PPP) Adjustment**
- US $50/month might need to be **$30-35/month in Chile**
- ~3x adjustment for lower purchasing power markets
- **"$10/month difference can break a deal"** for SMBs
[Source: Monetizely, SaaS Pricing Localization 2025]

✅ **Local Currency Pricing**
- Display in **Chilean Peso (CLP)**
- Transparent, tax-inclusive pricing builds trust
- Hedge against currency fluctuations
[Source: Kairos Aureum, LatAm SaaS Expansion 2025]

✅ **Billing Flexibility**
- Offer **monthly AND annual** options (42% offer both)
- Annual discount: 15-20% typical
- SMBs in LATAM prefer **monthly** initially (lower commitment)

### What SMB Retail Will Pay

**Current Spending Patterns:**
- SMBs spend **$100-300/employee/month** on SaaS tools
- **83% of LATAM SMBs** actively investing in digital
- **40% cite budget constraints** as #1 barrier
- **60% of Brazilian SaaS companies** recover CAC in 6 months (fast payback)
[Sources: Monetizely 2025, Crunchbase 2025, Kairos Aureum 2025]

**Recommended Pricing Strategy:**
